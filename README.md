# evacuation-drill-app
This application was designed to gather data from users who participated in drill events hosted by a team of researchers at OSU. Drills were conducted to better understand the levels of preparedness in tsunami evacuation zones along the Oregon coast.\
Mobile Flutter application to administer surveys and evacuation drill instructions while generating and uploading results.\

Drill Procedures Include:\
Inviting participants to participate in a drill event via downloading the mobile app on iOS or Android device.\
Participants will receive an invite code and meeting location on day of the event.\
Participants will then complete pre-drill surveys and begin the evacuation.\
Following drill completion, they will complete post-drill survey.\
Results will be sent to the researchers streamlining their info gathering and reduce delay in survey participation.
